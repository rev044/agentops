# AgentOps v2.27.0

## Highlights

The knowledge flywheel now tells you whether it's actually working. Four new **golden signals** measure whether your knowledge is compounding or just collecting dust — velocity trends, citation quality, research mining rates, and reuse distribution. Run `ao flywheel status --golden` to see where you stand.

## What's New

- **Golden signals for flywheel health** — `ao flywheel status --golden` shows four health indicators: is velocity increasing? Are citations useful? Is research being mined into learnings? Is the whole knowledge pool active or just a few items? Each signal has a verdict (compounding/accumulating/decaying) so you know exactly what to improve.

- **Automatic forge-to-pool bridge** — Knowledge extracted by forge now automatically writes pending learnings for pool ingestion, closing the last manual gap in the flywheel loop.

- **Session-start citation priming** — `ao lookup` runs at session start to surface relevant knowledge and create the citation events that drive the flywheel.

- **Skill catalog improvements** — Better descriptions and reference linking across the skill catalog.
